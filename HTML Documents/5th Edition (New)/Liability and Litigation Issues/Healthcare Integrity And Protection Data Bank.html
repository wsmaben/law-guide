Healthcare Integrity and Protection Data Bank

Information previously collected and disclosed through the HIPDB is now collected and disclosed through the National Practitioner Data Bank (NPDB). The HIPDB was a national health care fraud and abuse data collection program for the reporting, disclosing, and maintaining of a data base of final adverse actions taken against health care providers, suppliers, and practitioners. See NATIONAL PRACTITIONER DATA BANK (NPDB).
